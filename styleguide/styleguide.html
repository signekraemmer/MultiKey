Logo


This is the logo used for the MultiKEY app, shown on a white and black background.

They are arranged left to right, where left means they are more commonly used.


Button


In the Multikey app, there is only one call to action button.

This button is used as login-button, save-button etc.



Standard button

Insert text

Code to button

<button id="loginButton" class="button" type="button" name="?">Insert text</button>

Icon


Icons are used in forms such as username, password etc. so that people know exactly what they are filling out.

They are also used in the brugermenu and profile for more recognizability and quicker navigation.

Navigation


This is the bottom-navigation bar used in the Multikey App.

The navigation bar is made for Iphone X, but is responsive to all phone sizes.

The navigation bar is used on all pages, except login and sign up.
