FAQ

The app supports all electronic RFID applications and cards, and can contain ass many of these as the user wants.

For security reasons, the app is limited to one active key at any moment.

If you lose your phone or key chip, you can go to our website and temporarily suspend your account until you get a new chip. This way the keys cannot be used on your old chip.

A key can easily be unshared, by selecting the shared key from the key selection menu, and then select the share button, after which you can choose to stop sharing that key.
